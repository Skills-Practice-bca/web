Albert Einstein

Theoretical Physicist and Philosopher

About the Legend

Albert Einstein was a German-born theoretical physicist who developed the theory of relativity, one of the two pillars of modern physics.

  • He is best known for his mass–energy equivalence formula E = mc².
  • He was awarded the Nobel Prize in Physics in 1921.
  • He is considered one of the most influential scientists of the 20th century.
